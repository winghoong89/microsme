Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How is this different from my Facebook Page?

A microsite is your own professional digital asset. It's easily searchable on Google, offers more control over content, and builds greater credibility than just a social media profile. It acts as your permanent online home.

How long until my website is ready?

Typically 3-5 working days once we receive all the necessary information and content from you.

What if I don't have a logo or professional photos?

No problem! We can start with a simple text-based logo and use professional stock images as placeholders. You can always provide your own later to update your site!

What does '1 Minor Update Per Quarter' include?

This includes small text changes (e.g., updating operating hours, a new promo headline) or swapping a single image. Major redesigns or additional updates might incur a small additional fee, which we'll always discuss upfront.

Can I get my own .com.my domain later?

Currently, we focus on the `.microsme.store` domain to keep costs low and simplify your setup. If your business grows and you need your own `.com.my` in the future, we can discuss options for migrating your content to a new host and domain.

How do I pay?

You can securely pay the one-time setup fee and recurring monthly or annual payments via our online payment gateway.
